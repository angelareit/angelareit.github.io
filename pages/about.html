01 : About me

Hello There!

My name is Angela Tabafunda, an independent game developer currently based in Toronto, Ontario. I graduated from Ontario Tech University with a BSit. in Game Development and Entrepreneurship. I have worked various school projects and contracts, while doing self development on the side. The whole process of creating a game or an application excites me but my passion lies in UX/UI engineering and Technical Art. Creating interesting visual effects and creating efficiencies in workflows is thrilling, while implementing attractive and usable interfaces never fails to make me fuflfilled and happy. I also occasionally dabble in other game dev disciplines as well such as Game Programming, 3D modelling, and Game Design, since I would usually try to fill anything that a project needs. My diverse experience help me developed a wide range of skills to which enables me to quickly adapt and rise up from any challenge. If you would like to get in touch with me, feel free to send a message on LinkedIn or through email, I welcome any chance to connect!
